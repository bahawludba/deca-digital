FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What kind of AI assistants does Deca Digital build?

We build custom AI-powered chatbots and virtual assistants that handle customer inquiries, qualify leads, book appointments, and manage support tickets 24/7. They integrate seamlessly with your CRM, calendar, and communication channels including web chat, SMS, and email.

How much does a custom website cost?

Every project is unique. We offer tailored solutions based on your specific needs, brand, and goals. Contact us for a free discovery call and personalized proposal — no obligations. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

What is business automation and how can it help my company?

Business automation uses AI and smart workflows to handle repetitive tasks like lead capture, email follow-ups, invoicing, scheduling, and data entry — saving your team nearly a full day of work per week. Over 40% of work activities can be automated with current technology.

Does Deca Digital work with businesses outside of Texas?

Yes! While we're based in Texas, we serve clients nationwide and internationally. All our work is done remotely with regular video calls and progress updates. We've successfully delivered projects for clients across multiple states and countries.

How long does it take to build an AI assistant or website?

Timelines vary by project scope. A typical website takes 2-4 weeks. AI assistant integrations can be deployed in as little as 1-2 weeks for standard setups, or 4-6 weeks for fully custom solutions. We'll give you a clear timeline during our discovery call.
